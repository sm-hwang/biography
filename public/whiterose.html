You hack people, I hack time.

This image was supposed to be the logo for a time tracking application I had in mind. I never ended up starting the project for one reason or another, but liked the logo enough that I've adopted it for personal use.

Since creating the logo, I've realized that my passion for technology is to enable people to reach their full potential, to aid in the crusade against Resistance. I've also been inspired by the likes of Devine and others to create my own tools.

Whiterose, the Garden will be an ecosystem of keyboard driven, minimalistic productivity tools (see Tally for an example). I will design the tools primarily for my own use, but I have full confidence that others will find it useful as well.
